Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

SwapMonitor is a local macOS application that runs entirely on your device.

Data Collection

SwapMonitor does not collect any data. We do not gather personal information, usage statistics, system information, or any other data from users.

Network Access

SwapMonitor does not connect to the internet. The app operates entirely offline and does not make any network requests.

Local Data Storage

The app stores only your personal preferences locally using macOS UserDefaults. This includes settings such as notification preferences and window positions. No data is stored on external servers.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
